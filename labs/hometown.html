City Information

  • Country: South Korea
  • Area: 1,127.24 km2
  • Population: 497,197
  • Mayor: Kang-deok Lee

Pohang is a city in the province of North Gyeongsang, South Korea and a main seaport in the Daegu-Gyeongbuk region.

This city has the largest population of North Gyeongsang Province and it has the third-largest land area of any city in South Korea.
